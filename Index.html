My Project idea

Overview

My project idea is a cloud app work as a huge library include a hundred of apps that will help users can use other apps and programs without installing in their computer. This app can run on many devices such as in a computer, a tablet, a smartphone, a smart Television or even a Gameboy. People can use the apps just by several clicks on the screen and they do not need to worry about system requirements of products or the capacity of their computer. Basically, this app can help their users break the limit of hardware and freely enjoy technology.

Motivation

Nowadays, a system requirement is a huge problem for many people. Many apps ask their users to have a strong computer and special devices for use. Therefore, people must invest must money for their computer and buy other devices for working and entertaining. In my opinion, the hardware is the factor that defends people from using apps, so we need to find a way to solve this problem. Then, my idea comes up when I was watching a movie on Netflix and I thought that streaming service might be the best solution. Just in a few years, Netflix changes people’s habits from hiring CD, DVD to watch movie online from their devices. I believe that we can do the same thing with apps.

Description

This app requires nothing for their users but the strong and stable internet, I will explant some information and the way the app works. Firstly, I want to talk a little bit information about the app display, the apps will be work as a library of apps, all the apps will be a group in the major and their functions such as System- Security, Office, Multi-Media, Programming-Design, Applicable Software, Game and, Movie. Every product in this library will have a small box chat for users to evaluate and comment on their product. This app also has a ranking for products in each major. Furthermore, this app will have a tutorial for newbie and a 24/7 support system to answer and support users. Moreover, not only English this app will also support to translating to other languages. Basically, my app will work as a streaming video, while people use the app for example, they use my app to use Adobe Premiere. They have to find Adobe Premiere in Programming- Design group, then they will be moved to the working display screen. This app will be received all activities that users do in their devices in the working display screen and give feedback for the main server. Then, the main server will give that actions to another computer that have Adobe Premiere and do all the actions that user do in their devices. Then, the information from this computer will give to the main server and give feedback for the users’ devices. To be clearer, the users will work with another computer that has Adobe Premiere, their devices just play the role as a screen and receiving user’s interaction.

Tools and Technologies

The first step to make this app I have to create a big cloud database server that can store the information of users and their feedback – playing a role as cloud infrastructure. Then, I need to have a huge number of strong computers or a supercomputer for carrying out a hundred of apps for users. Moreover, I have to write programming to transfer information to the server. Therefore, this is also needed an agreement from many technology companies to allow their products in my app.

Skills Required

To build this app, I must have a huge range of knowledge of cloud computing and its service. Furthermore, it is needed to know programming languages relative to cloud computing such as Python, Java, ASP.NET, PHP and RUBY.

Outcome

I believe that this app is helpful for low-quality computer’s users and people who want to try many apps. If the app is successful, people can work in everywhere and on every device they want. They don’t need to worry about if this app suitable for their device or their device strong enough to run it, the only thing they have to give attention is the internet. Furthermore, I believe that my app can change the habit of customers while they buy a new device. They don’t need to buy a powerful computer for their work and a console device for their entertainment, all they need just an app can connect every app they want. Many ideas and projects in cloud service are failed in the past because of the limit of internet speed. But nowadays with the outstanding development of the internet speed, to be more specific 5G internet, I believe this is a favorable time for the cloud services like my app.
